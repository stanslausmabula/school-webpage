Admission

We welcome qualified students to join our school community. Our admission process is transparent and fair.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Previous academic records
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Health and vaccination records
  • Entrance examination (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Fill out the admission application form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Take the entrance examination
  4. Attend an interview
  5. Receive admission decision
